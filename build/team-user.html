As far back as I remember there was always a computer in our home – at first a massive thing with a green screen, into which you put floppies to play Space Invaders or Tetris. My first experience with the Internet came when I was around 13 years old.

But what really attracted me to computer science in general and InfoSec in particular was the way a program – software – that had a single intended purpose could be made to do something totally different once control over it was seized. That just sparked my imagination. I found it fascinating and mysterious. And it made me want to investigate things like that further.
